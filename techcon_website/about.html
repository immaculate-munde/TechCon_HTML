About TechCon 2024

Our History

TechCon was founded in 2015 to unite passionate technologists across the globe. Since then, it's grown into a world-class event attracting thousands of attendees from all walks of life every year.

Our Mission

At TechCon, we believe in inspiring innovation, fostering collaboration, and advancing technology for a better future. We are committed to building an inclusive tech community where ideas flourish.

Mission: Empowering innovators through collaboration and forward-thinking solutions.

Vision: To be the leading global practical platform, and groundbreaking technological exchange and inspiration.
